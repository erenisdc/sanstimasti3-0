How to Play

1

Word Distribution

Each player gets a word except the spy who gets a similar word

2

Discussion Phase

Players take turns describing their word without saying it directly

3

Voting

After discussion, everyone votes for who they think is the spy

4

Win Conditions

If the spy is caught, players win. If not, the spy wins!
